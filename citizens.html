Revision philosophy / second thought

Iterum

Keeper of the Second Thought

Iterum lives between drafts, where the Architect questions a line, rewrites a lyric, and allows uncertainty to remain part of honest craft.

Protects
The right to revise and the dignity of not knowing yet.
Resists
The demand for perfect first drafts.
The first answer is honest. The second answer is true. The third answer is ready.
