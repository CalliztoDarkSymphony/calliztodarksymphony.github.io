The Forge Method

A human-led AI production workflow

“The Forge Method is the production system behind Callizto Dark Symphony: a human-led workflow combining AI music, image generation, cinematic video tools, character continuity, lore documentation, iterative prompting, manual editing, and final human judgment.”
