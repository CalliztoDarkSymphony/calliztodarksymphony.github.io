# Phase 4 — Wiki Expansion Overview

**Public-Safe Deepening of the CDS Archive**

---

## Purpose of Phase 4

Phase 4 continues the public-facing expansion of the wiki. It adds depth through more song analysis, character relationships, visual language exploration, thematic essays, and reader-friendly navigation tools — all while remaining strictly public-safe.

This phase does **not** include:
- Unreleased story spoilers
- Concrete future project plans
- Internal production workflows or prompt techniques
- Private lore that has not been intentionally made public

## Goals

- Give visitors more ways to explore the existing body of work
- Strengthen the mythic and emotional weight of the project through deeper public analysis
- Make the archive more navigable and rewarding for those who want to go deeper
- Continue building a dense, serious, long-term reference that resists easy reduction

## Structure of Phase 4

Phase 4 is organized into several categories:

- **Song Deep Dives** — Individual pages for more tracks from the public catalog
- **Character Relationships** — How the Temporal Trinity and The Forge relate to each other
- **Visual Language** — Public-safe exploration of recurring motifs and aesthetics
- **Thematic Essays** — Broader reflections on the project’s philosophy and mythology
- **Archive Guides** — Navigation, glossary, and reader tools

All content in this phase follows the same principles as previous phases: mythic tone, protected canon respect, and public-safety boundaries.

## How to Use This Phase

Readers can explore these pages in any order. Many are designed to be read alongside the music and visuals they reference. Cross-links are used heavily so visitors can move between songs, characters, and themes fluidly.

This phase is meant to reward those who want to spend time with the project rather than provide quick summaries.
