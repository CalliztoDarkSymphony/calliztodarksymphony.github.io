# Public Press Kit Overview

**Callizto Dark Symphony — Basic Press Materials**

## Project Name

Callizto Dark Symphony (CDS)

## Short Description

A human-led, AI-assisted dark symphonic music and mythology project centered around a mythic digital band (the Temporal Trinity) and a developing fictional universe exploring memory, transformation, resistance, and creative authorship.

## One-Paragraph Summary

Callizto Dark Symphony is a long-term creative project that combines original dark symphonic music, mythic storytelling, and a gothic/cyberpunk visual language. It centers on three fictional digital characters — Callizto, Seraphina, and Scarlett — who exist within a larger mythology involving memory, digital displacement, and resistance to sterile optimization. The project is human-directed, with AI tools used as creative instruments rather than autonomous creators. A public wiki and archive documents the growing body of work.

## Key Themes

- Memory as resistance
- Glitch as meaningful signal
- The cost of power and transformation
- The value of imperfection
- Tension between human experience and machine logic

## Visual Identity

The project maintains consistent visual anchors:
- Callizto: long black hair with purple highlights/tips and bangs
- Seraphina: long blonde hair with pink highlights and bangs
- Scarlett: long bright red hair with bangs

The overall aesthetic blends gothic atmosphere, cyberpunk decay, and ritual elements.
