# Song Map by Theme

**Public Guide to Thematic Listening**

---

## Memory and Resistance

Tracks that explore memory (ancestral, personal, or embodied) as an act of defiance against erasure or optimization. These often carry emotional weight and mythic resonance.

**Related Concepts**: Memory as Resistance, The Misalignment, Digital Displacement

## Transformation and Rebirth

Tracks dealing with breaking down, fire, ash, and the decision to rise again. These often involve themes of cost, agency, and becoming.

**Related Concepts**: Transformation and Rebirth, The Pulse, Glitch as Feature

## Systems, Control, and Rebellion

Tracks that examine technology, systems, and structures as both oppressive and potentially rebellious. Precision, coding, and signal often appear as motifs.

**Related Concepts**: The Forge, Seraphina’s perspective, coded aesthetics

## Physicality, Rhythm, and The Pulse

Tracks that emphasize raw physical presence, rhythm, impact, and defiant continuation. These often feel more immediate and visceral.

**Related Concepts**: The Pulse, Scarlett’s perspective, ritual energy

## Grief, Weight, and Emotional Honesty

Tracks that carry heavy emotional material without offering easy resolution. These reward listeners willing to sit with difficulty.

**Related Concepts**: Memory as Resistance, human emotional truth

## Glitch, Imperfection, and Signal in Noise

Tracks that treat error, static, rupture, and imperfection as meaningful rather than as failures to be cleaned.

**Related Concepts**: Glitch as Feature, Digital Displacement

---

This map is not exhaustive. Many tracks contain multiple themes. Use it as a starting point for thematic exploration. Cross-reference with the Song Atlas (Phase 4) for deeper dives into individual tracks.
