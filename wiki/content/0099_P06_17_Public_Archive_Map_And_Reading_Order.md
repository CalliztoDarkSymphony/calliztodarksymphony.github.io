# Public Archive Map and Reading Order

**Suggested Paths Through the Wiki**

---

## For Complete Beginners

1. Start with Phase 5 (especially the one-page summary and short FAQ).
2. Listen to a few recommended tracks (My Digital Requiem, Beautiful Disgrace, Heart of the Static, Sanctuary of Scars).
3. Read the character entry guides in Phase 6.
4. Explore the Glossary for Newcomers (Phase 5) and core concept explanations (Phase 6).
5. Move into deeper pages in Phases 1–4 as interest develops.

## For Listeners Who Want More Music Context

Use the Song Maps in Phase 6 (by theme, character, and emotional entry point) alongside the Song Atlas in Phase 4.

## For Reviewers and Researchers

Start with the Long Artist Bio and Reviewer FAQ in Phase 5, then use the Song Maps, concept explanations, and archive map in Phase 6 to navigate the deeper material efficiently.

## For Long-Term Readers

The full MASTER structure (Phases 1–6) is designed for repeated engagement over time. There is no requirement to read everything in order. Follow what feels alive.

## General Principle

Listen first. Read second. Return to both. The wiki supports and deepens the music rather than replacing it.

---

This map is a public suggestion only. There is no single correct way to move through the archive.
