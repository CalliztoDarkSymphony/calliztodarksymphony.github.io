# Gothic Cyberpunk Visual Style Guide

**Public Overview of the Visual Language**

---

## Core Aesthetic Pillars

The visual language of Callizto Dark Symphony rests on three intersecting aesthetics:

**Gothic**
- Weight, solemnity, and inherited memory
- Dramatic lighting and theatrical presence
- Sense of ancient or ruined grandeur

**Cyberpunk**
- Decaying technology and beautiful ruin
- Glitch, static, and corrupted interfaces
- Neon and signal elements cutting through darkness

**Ritual**
- Ceremonial movement and intentional space
- Sacred or corrupted geometry
- Performance as transformation or invocation

## How They Combine

The most distinctive visuals occur where these three aesthetics overlap — gothic cathedrals of code, ritual circles in decaying industrial spaces, neon light in solemn environments. This combination creates a visual identity that feels both ancient and futuristic, broken and intentional.

## Character Visual Anchors

- Callizto: long black hair with purple highlights/tips and bangs, commanding gothic presence
- Seraphina: long blonde hair with pink highlights and bangs, precise cyber-goth styling
- Scarlett: long bright red hair with sharp bangs, powerful physical and ritual presence

These anchors remain stable even as smaller details may shift due to Digital Displacement.
