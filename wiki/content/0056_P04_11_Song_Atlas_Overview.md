# Song Atlas Overview

**Mapping the Public Catalog**

---

## Purpose

The **Song Atlas** is a growing collection of public-safe deep-dive pages for tracks from the Callizto Dark Symphony catalog. Each entry explores how a song connects to the larger mythology, characters, and themes without revealing private future plans or internal production details.

This is not a complete database of every track ever made. It focuses on publicly released or shared material and expands over time as new work becomes visible.

## How to Use the Song Atlas

- Start with the **Song Atlas Overview** for context
- Use individual **Character Song Atlases** (Callizto, Seraphina, Scarlett) to explore tracks strongly tied to each member
- Cross-reference with **Glossary** entries and **Thematic Essays** for deeper connections
- Listen to the music while reading — the Atlas is meant to accompany the listening experience, not replace it

## Current Structure

- **Overview** (this page)
- **Callizto Song Atlas** — Tracks strongly tied to Callizto’s perspective, burden, and transformation arcs
- **Seraphina Song Atlas** — Tracks that explore systems, precision, rebellion, and coded resistance
- **Scarlett Song Atlas** — Tracks that channel The Pulse, physical defiance, ritual, and embodied resistance
- Individual deep-dive pages for major tracks (e.g., *My Digital Requiem*, *Beautiful Disgrace*, *Heart of the Static*, *Sanctuary of Scars*)

## What the Atlas Does Not Include

- Unreleased story spoilers or future narrative arcs
- Concrete future project plans
- Internal production workflows or prompt techniques
- Private lore that has not been intentionally made public

## Growth

The Song Atlas will continue to expand as new public material is released and as deeper analysis is added for existing tracks. It is designed to grow alongside the project’s visible output.
