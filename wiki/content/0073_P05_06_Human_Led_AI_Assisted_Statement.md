# Human-Led, AI-Assisted Statement

**Callizto Dark Symphony — Creative Approach**

---

Callizto Dark Symphony is a **human-led, AI-assisted** creative project.

Human vision, direction, emotional judgment, selection, refinement, and final authorship are central to the work. AI tools are used as powerful creative instruments to expand what is possible in music generation and visual creation, but they do not replace human creative responsibility.

The project maintains consistent character identities, visual anchors, thematic continuity, and emotional intent across years of development. Decisions about what to keep, what to refine, what to discard, and what meaning to assign remain human.

This approach treats AI as a tool that can accelerate and expand creative possibilities, while insisting that human authorship, emotional truth, and long-term artistic responsibility stay irreplaceable.

Callizto Dark Symphony is fictional in structure (a mythic digital band and developing mythology) but emotionally and thematically grounded in real human concerns around memory, grief, transformation, resistance, and creative persistence.

The project does not position AI as the author or the primary creative force. It positions AI as a collaborative instrument within a human-directed artistic process.

---

This statement is intended for public clarity regarding the creative process and authorship of the project.
