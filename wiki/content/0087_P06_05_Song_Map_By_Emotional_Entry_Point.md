# Song Map by Emotional Entry Point

**Public Guide for Different Listener Moods**

---

## When You Want Emotional Weight and Depth

Tracks that carry grief, memory, transformation, and the cost of power. These often feel heavy but meaningful.

**Suggested Starting Points**: My Digital Requiem, Sanctuary of Scars, Heart of the Static

## When You Want Energy, Impact, or Catharsis

Tracks with strong rhythm, physical presence, and defiant continuation. These can feel more immediate and visceral.

**Suggested Starting Points**: High-energy ritual tracks, tracks with strong Pulse energy

## When You Want Atmosphere and Worldbuilding

Tracks that emphasize environment, mood, symbolic imagery, and the feeling of the larger fictional universe.

**Suggested Starting Points**: Atmospheric and worldbuilding-focused tracks

## When You Want to Explore a Specific Character

Use the Song Map by Character (previous page) to find tracks aligned with Callizto, Seraphina, or Scarlett.

## When You Want to Understand Core Concepts

Pair music with the concept explanation pages in this phase (The Misalignment, Digital Displacement, etc.) and the Glossary sections in earlier phases.

## General Advice

There is no wrong way to listen. Start where the music feels alive to you. The wiki exists to support and deepen the listening experience, not to replace it.

---

This map is designed to help new listeners find an emotional entry point that matches their current state or interest.
