# CDS FAQ for Reviewers and Journalists

**Frequently Asked Questions for Media and Press**

---

## What is Callizto Dark Symphony?

It is a human-led, AI-assisted dark symphonic music and mythology project. It combines original music, a developing fictional mythology, and a gothic/cyberpunk visual language centered around a mythic digital band called the Temporal Trinity.

## How would you describe the project in one sentence?

A human-directed creative archive combining dark symphonic music, mythic storytelling, and a consistent visual language, built over years with deliberate artistic vision.

## Is this an AI-generated project?

No. The project is human-led. Human vision, emotional direction, selection, and final authorship are central. AI tools are used as creative instruments, but they do not replace human creative responsibility or judgment.

## What kind of music do you make?

Dark symphonic, gothic, industrial, and hybrid styles with cinematic and atmospheric qualities. The music often explores heavy emotional and thematic territory.

## Who are the band members?

The Temporal Trinity consists of three fictional/digital characters: Callizto Lyhdan, Seraphina Veridian Cipher, and Lady Lilith Scarlett. They have distinct visual identities, personalities, and thematic roles within the project’s mythology.

## Do you consider this “AI art”?

The project uses AI tools as part of the creative process, but it is not defined by that fact. It is better understood as a human-directed music and mythology project that uses modern tools to realize a specific artistic vision.

## Why is there such a large public wiki?

The wiki serves as a public archive and reference for the project’s mythology, characters, songs, and themes. It exists for listeners, readers, and researchers who want deeper context. It is optional, not required.

## Is the mythology “canon”?

The wiki distinguishes between protected canon facts (locked for consistency) and elements that remain fluid or open to interpretation. Not everything is fixed.

## Where should new listeners start?

Good entry points include *My Digital Requiem*, *Beautiful Disgrace*, *Heart of the Static*, and *Sanctuary of Scars*. The wiki can be explored afterward if desired.

## Do you have a press kit?

A basic public press kit overview is available in the wiki (see Phase 5). More detailed materials can be requested through official project channels.
