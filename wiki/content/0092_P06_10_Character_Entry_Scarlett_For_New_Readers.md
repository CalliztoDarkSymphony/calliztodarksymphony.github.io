# Character Entry: Scarlett for New Readers

**Public Introduction**

---

## Who Is Scarlett?

Lady Lilith Scarlett is one of the three members of the Temporal Trinity. She embodies **The Pulse** — raw physical power, rhythm, impact, and defiant continuation. Her energy is often explosive, unapologetic, and deeply physical.

## Visual Identity (Stable Anchors)

- Long bright red hair with sharp bangs
- Powerful gothic presence tied to The Pulse
- Athletic, commanding physicality with latex-goth and ritual elements

Red is the color of raw life force and The Pulse.

## Thematic Role

Scarlett provides momentum and physical presence. She prevents reflection from becoming paralysis and brings impact, rhythm, and ritual energy. She is often associated with high-energy, ritualistic, or cathartic tracks.

## For New Listeners

Tracks aligned with Scarlett often feel more immediate, visceral, and rhythmically driven. They can provide energy and catharsis and pair well with exploration of The Pulse and embodied resistance.

## Relationship to the Larger Mythology

Scarlett’s energy connects with disruption, continuation, and the refusal to be stilled. She often intersects with Nyx’s disruptive function within The Forge.
