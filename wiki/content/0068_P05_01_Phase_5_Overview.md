# Phase 5 Overview

**Public Entry Layer for New Readers**

---

## Purpose of Phase 5

Phase 5 is the public-facing entry point into the Callizto Dark Symphony project. It is designed for people who have never encountered the project before — new listeners, reviewers, journalists, bloggers, music communities, and curious readers who want a clear, honest introduction.

This layer functions as the “front door” to the larger wiki and archive. It explains what the project is, how it works at a high level, and where to begin, without assuming prior knowledge.

## What Phase 5 Is

Phase 5 contains:
- Clear project summaries
- Short and long artist/project biographies
- Explanations of the human-led, AI-assisted nature of the work
- Public FAQs for different audiences (new listeners and reviewers/journalists)
- Guidance on where to start listening and reading
- A public glossary for newcomers
- A neutral, factual timeline summary

## What Phase 5 Is Not

Phase 5 does **not** contain:
- Private platform matters or disputes
- Internal creative processes or production secrets
- Unreleased story details or future plans
- Defensive or complaint-oriented language
- Fake reviews, fake quotes, or manufactured credibility

## Tone and Framing

Phase 5 uses clear, serious, and human language while staying aligned with the mythic identity of the project. It avoids reducing Callizto Dark Symphony to simple labels such as “AI music videos” or “an AI-generated band.”

Preferred framing includes:
- Human-led, AI-assisted dark symphonic music and mythology project
- Mythic digital band
- Public-facing digital band within the CDS universe
- Fictional in structure, emotionally real in function

## How to Use Phase 5

New readers can start with the shorter summaries and FAQs, then move into the deeper wiki (Phases 1–4) when they want more detail. Reviewers and journalists may find the longer bio, public press kit overview, and reviewer FAQ especially useful.

Phase 5 is designed to be useful on its own while also serving as a gateway into the richer archive behind it.
