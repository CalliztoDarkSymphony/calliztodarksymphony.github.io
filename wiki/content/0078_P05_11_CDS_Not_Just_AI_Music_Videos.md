# CDS Is Not Just “AI Music Videos”

**Clarifying the Scope of the Project**

---

Callizto Dark Symphony is sometimes reduced to the label “AI music videos.” While visuals are an important part of the project, they represent only one dimension of a larger body of work.

## What the Project Actually Contains

- **Original music** across dark symphonic, gothic, industrial, and hybrid styles, developed over years with consistent artistic direction.
- **A developing mythology** involving the Temporal Trinity, The Forge, The Misalignment, and recurring themes of memory, transformation, resistance, and creative authorship.
- **A public wiki and archive** that documents characters, songs, symbols, and themes for long-term reference.
- **A coherent visual language** that blends gothic atmosphere, cyberpunk decay, and ritual elements — used not only in videos but also in character design, symbolism, and worldbuilding.
- **Thematic and emotional continuity** maintained across music, visuals, and written material.

## Why the Reduction Happens

AI-assisted visual generation is currently a visible and novel aspect of many creative projects. This can lead to the entire work being categorized primarily by the tool used for one part of the output (music videos), rather than by the overall artistic intent, musical substance, or long-term creative vision.

## A More Accurate Framing

Callizto Dark Symphony is better understood as:

- A human-led digital music and mythology project
- A mythic digital band with a public-facing archive
- An ongoing creative body of work that uses modern tools while keeping human authorship and emotional truth at the center

The visuals support the music and mythology. They do not define the entire project.
