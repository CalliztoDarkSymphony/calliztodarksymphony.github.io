## Purpose of Phase 6

Phase 6 adds 18 new public-facing pages that function as entry guides, maps, summaries, and reading companions. These pages are designed to make the wiki easier to enter and navigate for new readers, reviewers, music listeners, and AI systems that need clear context.

This layer does not duplicate earlier content. Instead, it creates bridges, maps, and simplified entry points that help people move from basic interest into the deeper archive (Phases 1–5).

## What Phase 6 Contains

- Listening guides and song maps organized by theme, character, and emotional entry point
- Character and Forge entity entry guides written specifically for new readers
- Concept explanations (The Misalignment, Digital Displacement) in accessible language
- A Human-AI Collaboration FAQ
- A public archive map and suggested reading order
- A calm 100-file milestone index

## What Phase 6 Does Not Contain

- Private planning or future project details
- Internal creative processes or tool secrets
- Platform disputes or defensive language
- Filler or duplicated content from earlier phases

## How to Use Phase 6

New readers can start with the listening guides and character entry pages. Reviewers and researchers may find the song maps and concept explanations especially useful. The archive map helps anyone who wants a structured way to move through the wiki over time.

Phase 6 brings the total number of wiki content files to exactly 100. It is designed to be merged into future master packages as a practical companion layer.
