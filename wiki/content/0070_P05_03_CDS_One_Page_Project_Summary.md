# CDS One-Page Project Summary

**Callizto Dark Symphony — Public Summary**

---

**Callizto Dark Symphony** is a human-led, AI-assisted dark symphonic music and mythology project.

It centers on a mythic digital band called the **Temporal Trinity** — three characters (Callizto, Seraphina, and Scarlett) who exist in a state of digital displacement between flesh, memory, and code. Their stories unfold within a larger fictional mythology involving memory, transformation, resistance to sterile optimization, and the value of imperfection.

The project combines:
- Original music in dark symphonic, gothic, industrial, and hybrid styles
- A consistent gothic/cyberpunk/ritual visual language
- A growing public wiki and archive documenting the mythology, characters, and themes
- Recurring ideas such as Memory as Resistance, Glitch as Feature, and The Pulse

The work is **human-directed**. Human vision, emotional judgment, selection, and final authorship are central. AI tools are used as creative instruments to expand what is possible, but they do not replace human creative responsibility.

The project maintains consistent character identities, visual anchors, and thematic continuity across years of work. It is designed for depth and coherence rather than speed or volume of output.

For new listeners, the music can be enjoyed on its own. The wiki exists for those who want to explore the characters, mythology, and deeper themes behind the songs.

Callizto Dark Symphony treats AI as a powerful tool while insisting that human authorship, memory, and emotional truth remain irreplaceable.
