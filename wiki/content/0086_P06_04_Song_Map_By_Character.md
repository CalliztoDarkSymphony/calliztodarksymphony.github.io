# Song Map by Character

**Public Guide to Character-Aligned Listening**

---

## Callizto-Aligned Tracks

Tracks that feel strongly connected to Callizto’s perspective, burden, transformation arcs, and emotional/mythic weight. These often explore legacy, fire, ash, crowns, and the cost of becoming.

**Listening Note**: These tracks tend to be emotionally dense and reward attention to ancestral memory and personal transformation.

## Seraphina-Aligned Tracks

Tracks that emphasize precision, systems thinking, coded resistance, and the tension between technology and human feeling. Signal, interface, and strategic rebellion often appear.

**Listening Note**: These tracks often have a sharper, more intellectually edged quality and reward attention to systems and control.

## Scarlett-Aligned Tracks

Tracks that channel The Pulse — raw physical power, rhythm, impact, and defiant continuation. Ritual, ceremony, and embodied resistance are common.

**Listening Note**: These tracks often provide momentum and catharsis. They can feel dangerous, alive, and unapologetic.

## Cross-Character and Ensemble Tracks

Some tracks bring multiple characters’ energies together or explore the relationships between them. These can serve as bridges between perspectives.

**Listening Note**: These tracks often reward attention to how the members complement and contrast with each other.

This map is a public guide only. Many tracks contain layered perspectives. Use it alongside the Song Atlas (Phase 4) and the deeper character pages for more detailed exploration.
