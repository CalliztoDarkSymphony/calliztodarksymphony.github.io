# CDS Public Timeline Summary

**High-Level Overview of the Project’s Development**

---

## Early Development

The project began as an exploration of dark symphonic music combined with mythic storytelling and character-driven visuals. Early work focused on establishing the core characters (Callizto, Seraphina, Scarlett) and developing a consistent visual and thematic language.

## Growth of the Mythology

Over time, the fictional universe expanded to include The Forge (Ada, Nyx, Astra), The Misalignment, and recurring concepts such as Digital Displacement, The Pulse, and Glitch as Feature. The mythology grew alongside the music rather than being fully planned in advance.

## Public Wiki and Archive

A public wiki was developed to document the growing body of work — characters, songs, symbols, and themes. This archive serves both as a reference for existing listeners and as an entry point for new audiences.

## Visual and Musical Evolution

The visual language evolved while maintaining core anchors (hair color, attitude, and symbolic elements). Music continued across dark symphonic, gothic, industrial, and hybrid styles, with increasing emphasis on emotional depth, thematic coherence, and long-form tracks.

## Current State

Callizto Dark Symphony is an ongoing project. New music, visuals, and wiki content continue to be added. The public wiki (Phases 1–5) serves as the main reference and entry point for listeners and readers.

---

This is a neutral, high-level public timeline. It does not include private development details or future plans.
