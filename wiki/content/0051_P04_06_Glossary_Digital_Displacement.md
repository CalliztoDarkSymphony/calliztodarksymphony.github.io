# Glossary: Digital Displacement

**Core Concept**

---

## Definition

**Digital Displacement** is the fundamental state in which the central characters of Callizto Dark Symphony exist. They are translated beings suspended between flesh, memory, and code — not fully any one of these states, but existing in the unstable space between them.

This is not a temporary condition or a flaw to be fixed. It is their ontology — the condition of their being.

## Key Characteristics

- **Visual Instability**: Exact appearance can shift between videos and images. This is intentional canon.
- **Temporal Layering**: Characters carry residues from different timelines.
- **Emotional Cost**: Existing between states has psychological and existential weight.
- **Identity Anchors**: Hair color and core attitude remain the strongest stable elements.

## What It Allows

Digital Displacement permits subtle continuity drift in small details:
- Minor texture or material variation
- Small changes in trim, seams, or ornament placement
- Slight shifts in symbol or pattern details
- Nail color or minor accessory variation
- Tiny proportion or linework drift between generations

## What It Does NOT Allow

Digital Displacement is **not** permission for redesign. It does not permit:
- Hair color changes
- Core hairstyle changes
- Age or body type changes
- Face identity changes
- Full outfit replacement
- Using “drift” as an excuse for broken continuity or major redesign

## Relationship to Glitch

Digital Displacement naturally produces glitch. The instability of existing between states manifests as visual and narrative glitch. The project treats this glitch as meaningful signal rather than failure.

## Why It Matters

Digital Displacement is one of the project’s most important contributions to thinking about identity under technological pressure. It rejects the fantasy of perfect, lossless translation between human experience and digital representation. It embraces the fracture as a site of meaning, resistance, and strange new forms of existence.

It is also a direct counter to The Misalignment’s demand for clean, stable, optimized forms. Characters who exist in displacement cannot be easily contained or erased by systems that require perfect consistency.

## Guardrail

Digital Displacement allows subtle continuity drift in minor details.  
It does **not** allow changes to core visual anchors (hair color, hairstyle silhouette, face identity, body type).  
Core anchors remain protected. Drift belongs to the small things.
