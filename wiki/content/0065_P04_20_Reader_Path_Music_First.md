# Reader Path: Music First

**For Those Who Want to Start with the Songs**

---

## Recommended Path

This path is designed for listeners who want to experience the music first and let the wiki deepen their understanding over time.

### Step 1: Core Emotional Anchors
Listen to these tracks while reading their deep-dive pages:
- **My Digital Requiem**
- **Beautiful Disgrace**
- **Heart of the Static**
- **Sanctuary of Scars**

These four tracks give a strong introduction to the project’s emotional range, mythic tone, and core themes (The Misalignment, Digital Displacement, Glitch as Feature, Memory as Resistance).

### Step 2: Character Introduction
Listen to tracks strongly associated with each member while reading their character pages:
- Callizto-focused tracks (Crimson Legacy arc material, heavy emotional songs)
- Seraphina-focused tracks (systems, precision, coded rebellion)
- Scarlett-focused tracks (The Pulse, ritual, physical impact)

### Step 3: Thematic Exploration
Move into the **Glossary** and **Thematic Essays** while continuing to listen. Pay special attention to:
- The Misalignment
- Digital Displacement
- Glitch as Feature
- Memory as Resistance
- Transformation and Rebirth

### Step 4: Visual Language
Explore the **Visual Language** pages and **Symbolism Dictionary** while watching the music videos. Notice how hair anchors, color signatures, and recurring motifs (frost, ash, static, ritual geometry) support the themes.

### Step 5: Deeper Mythology
Read the full **Mythology** section and the **Forge** pages. Return to songs you already know and see how they connect to the larger web of relationships between the Temporal Trinity and The Sisters.

## Tips for This Path

- Don’t try to read everything at once. Let the music lead.
- Follow cross-links when a song or concept feels alive to you.
- Revisit pages after listening to related tracks — meaning accumulates.
- The wiki is meant to be returned to over time, not consumed in one sitting.

---

**The music is the primary experience. The wiki is a companion that grows richer the more you listen.**
