# Selected Works — Where to Start

**Guidance for New Listeners and Readers**

---

## Music Recommendations

For listeners new to the project, these tracks offer a strong introduction to different aspects of Callizto Dark Symphony:

**Emotional and Thematic Depth**
- My Digital Requiem
- Sanctuary of Scars
- Heart of the Static

**High-Energy / Ritual Energy**
- Welcome to Hell (both versions)
- We Are Warriors

**Mythic / Atmospheric**
- Narvas Isvindar / Narvas Icewinds
- Rise From The Ashes

**Character-Focused**
- Tracks connected to Callizto’s perspective and transformation arcs
- Tracks with strong rhythmic and physical presence (Scarlett-aligned)
- Tracks exploring systems and precision (Seraphina-aligned)

## Wiki Recommendations

After listening, new readers may find these sections helpful:

1. Start with the **Phase 5** pages (especially the one-page summary and short FAQ).
2. Read the character pages for Callizto, Seraphina, and Scarlett.
3. Explore the **Glossary** entries for core concepts (The Misalignment, Digital Displacement, Glitch as Feature, The Pulse).
4. Use the **Song Atlas** and individual song deep-dives for tracks you enjoyed.

## Suggested Listening Order (One Possible Path)

1. My Digital Requiem
2. Beautiful Disgrace
3. Heart of the Static
4. Sanctuary of Scars
5. A Callizto-focused track from the Crimson Legacy material
6. A high-energy/ritual track

Listen first. Read second. Return to both as interest grows.

---

There is no single “correct” way to enter the project. Start where the music or ideas feel alive to you. The wiki is here when you want more context.
